Who We Are

Mtendele Baptist Church is a Christ-centered community in Lilongwe, Malawi, committed to biblical truth, spiritual growth, and serving our neighbors with Christ's love.

Quality Ministry

We strive for excellence in all we do, ensuring our worship, teaching, and service meet the highest standards to honor God.

Democratic Leadership

Our church operates with congregational governance, where members prayerfully make decisions together under Christ's lordship.

Transparent Service

We maintain open communication and accountability in all our ministries and financial matters as we serve God and our community.

We are Mtendele Baptist

As the name suggests itself - "Kwanthu ndi kumwamba indeee! Koma timasonkhana pa mtendlepo."

We are a community of believers who gather at Mtendele to worship, grow, and serve together. Our church is:

  • Bible-based - We stand firmly on the Word of God as our foundation for faith and practice, with transparent teaching you can trust.
  • Christ-centered - Everything we do is focused on glorifying Jesus Christ through democratic processes that value each member's voice.
  • Spirit-led - We depend on the Holy Spirit to guide and empower our ministry while maintaining accountability to one another.
  • Mission-minded - We're committed to sharing the Gospel in our community and beyond with excellence and integrity.

Our Baptist Distinctives

Biblical principles that define our church's democratic governance and transparent operations

1

Biblical Authority

The Bible is our sole authority for faith and practice, containing all truth necessary for salvation and Christian living.

2

Autonomy of the Local Church

Each local church is self-governing under Christ's lordship, free from external hierarchy or control.

3

Priesthood of All Believers

Every Christian has direct access to God through Christ and shares in the ministry of reconciliation.

4

Two Ordinances

We practice believer's baptism by immersion and the Lord's Supper as symbolic acts of obedience.

5

Individual Soul Liberty

Every person has the right and responsibility to approach God directly and interpret Scripture for themselves.

6

Saved, Baptized Membership

Church membership is for those who have personally trusted Christ and followed in believer's baptism.
